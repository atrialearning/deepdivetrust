When Trust Feels Steady or Not

Think about a regular workday. Sometimes things feel clear and predictable. At other times, they feel uncertain, even if no one has done anything “wrong.”

Trust often shows up before we put words to it: in how decisions are communicated, in how changes are handled, and in how consistent responses feel over time.

This course starts here because trust is not only built through intentions or personalities. It is also shaped by what people experience repeatedly in their working environment.

You don’t need to analyze yourself. Just notice what feels familiar.
